Saga of The Clockmaker's Prophecy

Name: BELEN, Mariane
Year&Section: BSCS 2B
Subject: Web Development

Chapter 1: The last Clockmaker and the frozen Clock

In a quiet town of Odeses, every
clock, from the smallest pocket watch
to the giant tower bell is maintained
by a clockmaker, a master of time,
Severus Vale. He is the last
Clockmaker, unknown to most,
his work doesn't just keep time,
but keeps Time itself from unraveling.
One night, the townfolks wake to find
every clock frozen at exactly 11:07.
The clockmaker was gone, leaving behind
a strange brass key and a paper,
in an incomplete clock, and the note reads:
"When the twelveth hour strikes,
time will never move again."
Albus Phil,the clockmaker's apprentice
takes it upon himself to find the answers.

Chapter 2: Chronos and the war of The Keepers and Turners

While going around finding answers in
Severus workshop, he finds books and
journals. In those books and journals
he finds the word Chronos an ancient
machine that is said to control the
flow of time across the world. As he
flips more he finds flashbacks revealing
the war between two secret organizations,
The Keepers and Turners. He also learns
about the Twelveth Hour-a mythical moment
outside normal time. As he digs deeper a
masked man covered in black tries to
steal the brass key, and claims to
be a Turner. Albus fought to
protect it, but then suddenly the frozen
clock ticks once. But he doesn't have
a time to lose. Albus managed to escape
with the brass key.

Chapter 3: The Twelveth Hour

It was said that the Chronos is underneath
his Master's old Tower. He went there and
looks for it and saw it damaged and leaking glowing
sand. As he looks he saw his Master's figure
trapped. The clock was ticking it's almost time, he
is still confuse in what to do, but then a voice came.
Albus faced where the voice came from
and saw a tall man covered in black.
"Good evening to you young lad" Albus became
serious "Oh~ introduction I forgot. I am
Lucius Voldemort, a turner you see.
I would like to give you an offer.
You give me the key and I will give you your Master."
Albus refused. The clock was ticking
louder and louder. Suddenly he remembered the prophecy
written in his master's book and realizes that the
prophecy was about him dissapearing to restore the time
and save his master. Without hesitation he used the
brass key and entered the Chronos, repairing
it from within and breaking the loop, while the Turner
stood helpless because his plan was destroyed.
Severus is freed, time flows normally
again, but Albus vanished.
Severus still hopes that one day his apprentice
will return, that's why he still keeps a clock
frozen in 11:07 waiting.
