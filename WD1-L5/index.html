Palawan

The largest island in the Philippine province of Palawan is called Palawan
Island. It is a long island, the fifth largest in the Philippines, that runs
from northeast to southwest, with the Sulu Sea to the southeast and the South China
Sea to the northwest.

Boracay

The Philippines is home to the tropical paradise that is Boracay Island.
This vacation spot is a veritable piece of heaven on earth, with its white
sand beaches and pristine waters. However, Boracay is more complex than it first appears.

Hundred Islands

A Hundred Islands Tour is the ideal way to experience the park; on this tour, guests can hop from
island to island, swim in turquoise waters, snorkel among vibrant marine life, and enjoy breath-taking
views from island viewpoints.
